Lasagna

Great Italian Recipes

Ingredients

Ground Beef

Garlic Cloves

Marinara Sauce

Sugar

Parsley

Cottage, Ricotta Cheese, Mozzarella Sauce

Egg

Directions

Add oil to a deep pan and sautee onion and beef until browned, 5 minutes then add garlic and stir another minute. Add marinara, salt, pepper, thyme, sugar, and parsley; bring to a simmer, then cover and cook 5 minutes.

Combine all of the cheese sauce ingredients and 1 cup of mozzarella in a mixing bowl (reserve the rest for later).Preheat your oven to 375ºF and cook the lasagna noodles in a pot of well-salted water until al dente.

Spread 1/2 cup of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9×13-inch casserole dish. Add 3 noodles, followed by 1/3 of the meat sauce, 1 cup of mozzarella, and half of the ricotta mixture. Repeat, then for the top layer, add 3 noodles, 1/3 of the meat sauce, and the remaining mozzarella. Cover with foil and bake at 375˚F for 45 minutes, then remove the foil and broil for 3 to 5 minutes, or until the cheese is lightly browned.

Let the lasagna rest for about 30 minutes before cutting and serving.
